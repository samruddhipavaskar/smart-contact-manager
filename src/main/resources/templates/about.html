About SCM 2.0

Smart Contact Manager — your personal, cloud-powered address book.

What is SCM 2.0?

SCM 2.0 is a full-stack Spring Boot web application that lets you securely store, search, and manage all your personal and professional contacts in one place — accessible from anywhere.

Built with modern technologies including Spring Security, OAuth2 login (Google & GitHub), Cloudinary image hosting, and MySQL persistence, SCM 2.0 is designed to be secure, fast, and user-friendly.
